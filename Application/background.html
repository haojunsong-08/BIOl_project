Spatial Transcriptomes
A Great Way to Learn Cells

Spatial transcriptomics, with other spatial technologies, has enabled scientists to dissect the organization and interaction of different cell types within the tumor microenvironment. Read more…

Spatial transcriptomic techniques have existed for almost a decade, but until recently only at the institutes where they were developed. Commercialized techniques such as Spatial Transcriptomics, released as Visium by 10X Genomics, as well as GeoMx and CosMx by Nanostring, have made spatial transcriptomics more accessible. Other omics techniques such as ‘spatial proteomics’, spatial assays for chromatin accessibility, and spatial genomics have also contributed recent findings.
